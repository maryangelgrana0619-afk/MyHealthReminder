About
MyHealth Reminder
Version 1.0.0 · Your Daily Wellness Companion
A personal health management tool designed to help you stay consistent with hydration, medication, and exercise — building healthier habits one day at a time.
About the App

MyHealth Reminder is a mobile wellness companion that empowers individuals to take charge of their daily health routines. Whether you need to remember to drink water, take your medication, or squeeze in a workout, this app keeps you on track with smart reminders and daily checklists.

Track your progress over time with weekly charts, maintain streaks, and celebrate daily wins — all in a clean, easy-to-use interface designed for everyone.

Key Features
Smart Reminders
Schedule hydration, medication and exercise reminders with custom tones, frequency, and priority.
Daily Checklist
Stay on top of your wellness goals with a personalized daily checklist that resets each morning.
Progress Tracking
Visualize your weekly wellness performance with bar charts, streaks, and category breakdowns.
Secure & Private
All your health data stays on your device. No cloud sync, no data selling — your info is yours.
SDG Goals Alignment

MyHealth Reminder supports the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals by promoting personal health, inclusive wellness access, and quality health-related education through technology.

SDG 3
Good Health & Well-being
Promotes daily health habits including hydration, medication adherence, and physical activity for all ages.
SDG 4
Quality Education
Educates users on wellness routines, building health literacy and self-care awareness through daily reminders.
SDG 10
Reduced Inequalities
Provides free, accessible health tools that empower all individuals regardless of socioeconomic background.
SDG 17
Partnerships for the Goals
Built with technology to support collaborative health goals and encourage shared wellness progress.
